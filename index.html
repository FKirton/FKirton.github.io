About me

I am a PhD student in the Centre for Language Evolution at the University of Edinburgh, supervised by Simon Kirby, Kenny Smith, Jenny Culbertson and Marieke Schouwstra.

In my research, I investigate the cognitive biases that influence word order in improvised or newly developing communication systems. I am particularly interested in how features such as the animacy of the interacting entities affects the way people structure their descriptions of events.

I am funded by the Carnegie Trust for the Universities of Scotland.

Research interests: language evolution, word order, animacy & syntax, event conceptualisation, communication, emerging sign languages
